Phase 3 Oblique Explorer
Click on an airplane to find links to its imagery and metadata. Green polygons will be shown approximating the exposure extent for each oblique photograph. The center of the green polygons are the image centroids.
Search for a specific flight and shot number to find the oblique imagery you want to explore. For example, to find the exposures for Flight 7114 and Shot 6717, type the numbers separated by an underscore. 
7114_6717
885,789 exposures captured five shots each: nadir and the four cardinal directions. Because the vector tiles can not encode all exposure points at low zoom levels, you may need to zoom in to the map to see exposure sites.
